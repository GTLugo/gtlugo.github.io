Hello hello, friends!

Since my last blog post, I've actually graduated university with a degree in Computer Science! Still, actually finding a job in software development is a kind of hell that I wish upon no one. To help myself get more experience and projects under my belt, I'm thinking about publishing a game on Steam.

If you somehow found my itch.io page, you might actually know that I've had an idea for a game banging around in my head for a while now. Despite this, I've been struggling a lot finding the time and motivation to actualize it. Now that I am no longer in university, my time is slightly more free (but working in fast food is eating that up a little), so my main blocker is my motivation. Because of this, I'm thinking about trying something new: monthly blog posts.

My thought process is that if I set a goal for myself to post *at least* one blog post a month chronicling my trials and tribulations, I might be more motivated to make progress. As a bonus, it'd be fun to look back in a few years and see everything I have done.

As always, I make no actual promises about quality of the posts or frequency since I have been proven very unreliable with updates (see my 8 year old Terraria mod as proof).

Until next time...
